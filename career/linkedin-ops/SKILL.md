# LinkedIn Operations

Guidelines for LinkedIn content creation, profile optimization, and professional networking.

## Post Writing Framework

### Structure: Hook â†’ Value â†’ CTA

**Hook (Line 1-2):** Stop the scroll. Must be compelling enough to click "see more."
- Start with a bold statement, surprising stat, or contrarian take
- Use line breaks after the hook for visual separation
- Avoid clickbait â€” deliver on the promise

**Value (Body):** The substance. Teach, share, or tell a story.
- Use short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- Include line breaks between paragraphs
- Use bullet points or numbered lists for scanability
- Personal stories outperform generic advice
- Specific > vague (numbers, names, details)

**CTA (Last line):** Drive engagement.
- Ask a question ("What's your take?")
- Invite sharing ("Repost if this resonates")
- Offer something ("DM me for the template")

### Content Rules
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts per week maximum
- **Timing:** Tue-Thu 8-10am or 12-1pm (audience timezone)
- **Length:** 150-300 words (long enough for value, short enough to read)
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant ones, placed at the end
- **Engagement:** Reply to every comment within 24 hours
- **No:** Humblebrags, engagement bait, "agree?" posts, political hot takes

## Profile Optimization Checklist

### Headline (220 chars)
- [ ] Includes current role AND value proposition
- [ ] Uses keywords recruiters search for
- [ ] Format: `[Role] | [What you do/value] | [Specialty]`
- [ ] Example: `Security Engineer | Building secure cloud infrastructure | Kubernetes, AWS, Zero Trust`

### About Section (2600 chars)
- [ ] First 3 lines are compelling (visible before "see more")
- [ ] Written in first person
- [ ] Tells your story, not just lists skills
- [ ] Includes specific achievements with metrics
- [ ] Ends with what you're looking for or how to reach you
- [ ] Contains relevant keywords naturally

### Experience
- [ ] Each role has 3-5 bullet points
- [ ] Bullets start with action verbs
- [ ] Includes quantifiable results where possible
- [ ] Describes impact, not just responsibilities
- [ ] Rich media attached (presentations, articles, projects)

### Skills & Endorsements
- [ ] Top 3 skills aligned with target role
- [ ] 50+ skills listed (helps with search)
- [ ] Endorsements requested from credible connections

### Other
- [ ] Professional headshot (face fills 60% of frame)
- [ ] Custom banner image (not default)
- [ ] Custom URL (`linkedin.com/in/yourname`)
- [ ] Featured section with best content/projects
- [ ] Open to Work badge (if applicable â€” set to recruiters only)
- [ ] Creator mode ON (if posting regularly)

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Rhythm
- **Monday:** Industry insight or news commentary
- **Wednesday:** Technical deep-dive or tutorial
- **Friday:** Personal story, lesson learned, or career reflection

### Monthly Themes
- **Week 1:** Share a project or accomplishment
- **Week 2:** Teach something technical
- **Week 3:** Comment on industry trend
- **Week 4:** Personal/career reflection

### Engagement Tasks (Daily, 10 min)
- Comment thoughtfully on 3-5 posts in your feed
- Reply to all comments on your posts
- Accept/send 2-3 connection requests
